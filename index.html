Car commission

Sale of cars: 1% of the car value

Cars for assignment: 1% of the value of the assignment if the assignment is for a fee.

Car exchange: 1% of the exchange value if there is an exchange rate.

Commission for other goods and services

  • Selling an item: 1% of the value of the item sold
  • Lease of goods (equipment, etc.): 1% of the value of the rental amount.
  • Providing services: 1% of the value of the service provided. In the event that the service is provided to more than one person, 1% will be transferred for each service provided.
  • Request for a good or service: 1% of the sale value.
  • Real estate products and real estate services do not have a commission
